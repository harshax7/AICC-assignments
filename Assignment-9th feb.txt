1. Google Search

AI helps rank search results based on relevance. ML models analyze user queries, past searches, and website content to show the best results.

2. YouTube Recommendations

ML algorithms track watch history, likes, and time spent to suggest videos similar to user interests.

3. Netflix / OTT Platforms

AI recommends movies and shows using collaborative filtering based on user preferences and behavior patterns.

4. Google Maps Navigation

ML predicts traffic conditions using real-time data from users and suggests the fastest route.

5. Voice Assistants (Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri)

Speech recognition models convert voice to text, and NLP models understand intent and provide responses.

6. Email Spam Filtering (Gmail)

ML models classify emails as spam or not by analyzing patterns, keywords, and sender behavior.

7. Instagram / Facebook Feed

AI ranks posts based on engagement, likes, comments, and user interaction patterns.

8. Online Shopping (Amazon, Flipkart)

Recommendation systems suggest products based on browsing history, purchases, and similar users’ behavior.

9. Keyboard Auto-correct & Suggestions

ML models predict next words and correct spelling errors using language models trained on large text data.

10. Face Unlock in Smartphones

Computer vision models detect and match facial features with stored data to authenticate users.
