A decision tree is a flowchart-like structure used for decision-making. Each internal node represents a condition, each branch represents the outcome of the condition, and each leaf node represents a final decision.

In this example, the decision to play outside depends on weather conditions like sunlight, temperature, and wind. The tree helps systematically evaluate conditions to arrive at a conclusion.
